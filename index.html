Help The Ones in Need

Food industries can make a positive difference by giving their extra food to homeless shelters and food banks. This helps feed people who might otherwise go hungry. It's a simple way to show kindness and reduce food waste. Why throw away extra food when you can donate it to people in need? With HungerHeroes, food industries can donate their extra food to the ones in need all over the US to help eliminate hunger and food waste.
